    Enable history expansion with eshell

    For some reason, history expansion through ! notation is not enabled by default in Emacs eshell. The variable eshell-expand-input-functions needs to be extended as follows: Add the following to your configuration:

    (add-to-list 'eshell-expand-input-functions 'eshell-expand-history-references)
